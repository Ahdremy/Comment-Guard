Frequently Asked Questions

Is this software approved by Facebook and Instagram?

CommentGuard has been manually reviewed by the Meta App Review team and was approved. It's 100% compliant with Meta's policies so you'll never put your Facebook or Instagram account at risk by using our software for automated comment moderation.

How easy is it to integrate with CommentGuard?

After signing up you'll be asked to connect your Facebook account and select the Facebook Pages and Instagram Business Accounts you'd like to use with CommentGuard. Allowing CommentGuard to moderate comments on your behalf only takes a few clicks.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, you can try CommentGuard free when signing up for a trial account. During the trial you'll have full access to the software, so you can see if you like it or not. After 7 days your trial will expire automatically. No credit card is needed to start your free trial.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We only accept credit card payments.

What happens if I exceed my comments/mo?

Nothing, the software will keep working. We'll just reach out to you and ask you to upgrade depending on your comment volume.

What does 30 day data retention mean?

Comments on your posts and ads will be automatically imported into CommentGuard and saved in our database. We store these comments for 30 days and then erase them from our application.

Can I choose different moderation settings for organic vs paid?

CommentGuard moderation settings are specified at the level of each individual Facebook Page or Instagram Business Account. They're applied to all comments received on organic posts as well as paid ads. We don't support different moderation settings for organic posts vs paid ads, because there's an unclear overlap between these (organic posts can be boosted as sponsored posts for example).

Does this work for Facebook sponsored page posts?

CommentGuard supports automated moderation of comments on all types of Facebook and Instagram organic posts and ads, including sponsored Facebook page posts as well as ads created through Facebook Ads Manager. We also support stories and reels.

Which languages do you support?

Our AI filters support: English, Spanish, German, French, Arabic, Japanese, Hindi, Italian, Portuguese and Russian.

Do you import my comment history?

CommentGuard processes new comments, starting from the moment you add a Facebook or Instagram page to your account. It does not retroactively import comments that were received before the Facebook or Instagram page was added to our software.

Why do you hide instead of delete comments?

CommentGuard automatically hides comments based on your moderation settings, but doesn't automatically delete comments. This is on purpose and for two important reasons. Firstly, hiding a comment on Facebook means the author and his or her friends can still see it while at the same time it's hidden from the broad public. This prevents further outrage and the feeling of censorship, while limiting the public visibility of harmful comments. Secondly, hiding is a reversible action, meaning comments can still be made publicly visible again. You can manually unhide or delete comments that were hidden inside your dashboard.

Can I completely disable comments with CommentGuard?

You can enable our "private mode" setting, which will instruct CommentGuard to automatically hide all new comments within seconds of being posted. This way you're able to review comments and approve them before they'll be publicly visible.
